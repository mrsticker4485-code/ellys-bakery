Our Story

Two kitchens, one table

Elly's Bakery started as a home kitchen in Katy filling orders one cake at a time. Today we bake celebration cakes, cookies, and rolled pastries alongside a full menu of traditional Persian dishes — from saffron rice to slow-simmered khoresht — so you can order dessert and dinner from the same place.

Made to order — every cake, tray, and pot is baked or cooked after you order, not sitting on a shelf.
Two menus, one kitchen — Western-style bakery favorites and traditional Persian home cooking.
Simple ordering — text us or DM us on Instagram, then swing by Katy to pick it up.
